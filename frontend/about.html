About Your Dr.

EfficientNet-B4 Model

Model Architecture

  • Base Model: EfficientNet-B4
  • Input Size: 224x224 pixels
  • Classes: COVID-19, Pneumonia, Normal
  • Framework: PyTorch
  • Preprocessing: ImageNet normalization

Performance Metrics

  • Accuracy: ~95% on validation set
  • Precision: High for all classes
  • Recall: Optimized for medical use
  • Inference Time: < 2 seconds

How It Works

1

Image Upload

Users upload chest X-ray images in PNG, JPG, or JPEG format (max 10MB).

2

Preprocessing

Images are resized to 224x224 pixels and normalized using ImageNet standards.

3

AI Analysis

The EfficientNet-B4 model analyzes the image and provides classification probabilities.

4

Results Display

Results show predicted class, confidence level, and probability breakdown with animated bars.

Important Limitations

Not a Substitute for Professional Diagnosis: This AI system is designed to assist healthcare professionals, not replace them.

Limited to X-ray Images: The model is trained specifically on chest X-ray images and cannot analyze other types of medical imaging.

Training Data Constraints: Performance depends on the quality and diversity of the training dataset used.

Potential for Errors: Like all AI systems, this model can make incorrect predictions. Always verify results with clinical expertise.

Regular Updates Required: Medical AI models should be regularly updated with new data and validated against clinical outcomes.

Usage Guidelines

For Healthcare Professionals: Use this tool as a second opinion to support clinical decision-making.

Image Quality: Ensure X-ray images are clear, properly exposed, and focused on the chest area.

Patient Privacy: Never upload images containing patient identifiable information.

Follow-up Required: All AI predictions should be followed up with appropriate clinical tests and professional evaluation.

Report Issues: If you encounter consistently poor performance, please contact us for feedback.
